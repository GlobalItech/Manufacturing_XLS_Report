Manufacturing Report

Following Types of Reports can be obtained:
  • Grand Production Summary - Report List of manufactured products with quantity on given date/ period
  • Production Receipt and Consumption Report - Report List of Raw Material Consumed with quantity on given date/ period
  • Grand Production Summary Schedule Date - Report List of manufactured products with quantity on given date/ period as per scheduled date

There are several benefits of this manufacturing report.:

    Dates/Period Flexibility & multiple Report Types.
  • A report can be obtained of flexible dates. There is no binding on users to select a week, monthly or a year period. And you can also get reports of three different types which can be seen in the picture below.
    • Category and Product selection
  • Not only you can choose Specific category(s) but you can also choose specific product(s) in that category.
    • Warehouse & Location Wise
  • You can see the production of goods, warehouse and location wise.
    • Report
  • Grand Production Summary This report tells the Quantity produced in specific dates. Once Downloaded the report will tell you the required information in a very simple but informative form.
  • Production Receipt and Consumption Report This report tells quantity consumed for in production process in specific dates.
  • Grand Production Summary Schedule Date With this help of this report, we are able to know quantity of products scheduled to be produced in specific dates.
